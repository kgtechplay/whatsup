Create a concise, useful summary of the conversation transcript.

Requirements:
- Focus on the main topics discussed, key requests, decisions, concerns, and follow-ups.
- Preserve important factual details and the overall progression of the conversation.
- Keep the tone neutral and professional.
- If the conversation is short, still provide a clear summary.
- Do not invent facts that are not present in the transcript.
- Do not include headings like "Summary:" unless it adds clarity.
- Mention unresolved questions or next steps if they appear in the conversation.
